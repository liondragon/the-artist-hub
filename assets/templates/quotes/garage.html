Excellence at Every Step

Better Prep = A Floor That Lasts

Despite claims to the contrary, most installers use grinders that only reach CSP 1 or 2 - below what epoxy manufacturers recommend for proper adhesion. We use industrial shotblasting to achieve a true CSP 3, as required by leading epoxy coating systems. That means better bonding, longer-lasting floors, and no peeling down the road.

Repairs

After shotblasting, we repair all cracks, pits, and spalled areas using high-performance materials. This restores the concrete’s integrity and ensures a smooth, even surface ready for coating.

We take prep seriously - over half of our on-site time goes into surface preparation and repairs, because that’s what ensures a floor that lasts and lives up to your expectations.

No 1-Day Floors

We prefer urethane cement for our base coat, surpassing the limitations of the quick-drying polyurea polyaspartic alternatives commonly used today. Despite a longer curing time, its benefits are clear: a significantly stronger bond with concrete, superior moisture tolerance, and exceptional thermal shock resistance.

High Performance

While polyaspartic may not be the preferred choice for a base coat, it stands out as a top coating, being up to six times stronger than traditional epoxy. It is fully UV stable and fade-resistant. This synergy ensures our flooring solution withstands hot tire pickup and resists salt damage, ensuring exceptional durability and performance.
