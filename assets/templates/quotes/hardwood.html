Dust Containment

As a Bona Certified Craftsman, we employ specialized sanding equipment that creates 99% less dust compared to traditional sanding process. Having a cleaner and healthier working environment allows you to comfortably remain in your home throughout the entire refinishing project.

While 99% less dust is substantial, there is still that bit of a dust going airborne. To address this, we offer an additional dust containment service tailored to your needs. Our À La Carte options will let you decide which areas should be sealed of with protective plastic.

To further safeguard your home, we can cover cold air returns with electrostatic filters to stop dust from circulating through your heating system, if requested.

Final Cleaning

To ensure your home returns to its pristine state, we offer the option to engage one of our trusted partners for a comprehensive deep cleaning service upon completion of your project. This service encompasses the vacuuming of baseboards, dusting of walls, light fixtures, countertops, and cleaning of hardware, providing you with a spotlessly clean home.
